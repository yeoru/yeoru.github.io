Hello chaosmos.
Yang Yeong(Yeongju Yang) is a painter and media artist who is interested in interaction(transaction) and energy.





I expresses the various energies arising from the interaction between humans, technology, and the environment through painting and media art. Drawing inspiration from fields such as cybernetics, posthumanism, and life sciences, the artist conceptualizes and portrays energy in diverse abstract forms. In my work, the recurring motif of 'tentacles' serves as a key element in imagining these themes and exploring new possibilities.
